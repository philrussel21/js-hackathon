    About

    Long Shot is an interactive addicting game designed for basketball fans. The goal of the game is to score as much as you can. The app also has a side-feature to bring you the season average of your favourite player or view top performers of your favourite team. Bring up the stats of your favourite player or favourite team and try to outscore them!
